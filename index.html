-Bungo Stray Dogs

I loved this anime.However,the storytelling is an absolute mess-honestly,the plot is a complete disaster.I doubted whether the author even had a plot in mind or not.Perhaps the author lost her marbles while writing the story.

It's really funny how the author builds the images of Russian writers — like Pushkin and Gogol. It seems that, from the book's perspective, all these Russian writers are on the evil side. I can't help wondering whether the author had a really bad time reading those Russian writers' books, like Crime and Punishment. I can't think of any other reason why he would portray them that way.

Although I really enjoy seeing wholesome faces appearing on my screen, I stopped reading the comic — because it's simply nonsense. It's like the Cold War: all the characters, all the plots, are out of control. However, the characters are so handsome — that's fair enough. I will finish the comic someday.
