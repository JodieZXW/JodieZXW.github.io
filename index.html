-Bungo Stray Dogs

I loved this anime. However,the storytelling is an absolute mess---honestly, the plot is a complete disaster. My intelligence drains away with every word I read, I would rather be stabed than read the latest chapters. I doubted whether the author even had a plot in mind or not. Perhaps the author lost his marbles while writing the story.

It's really funny how the author builds the images of Russian writers,like Pushkin. I can't help wondering whether the author had a painfully bad time reading those Russian writers' books,for instance Crime and Punishment, of which I genuinely prefer. Apart from that, no other reason appears in my mind to why he would portray them like that.

Seeing wholesome faces appearing on my screen would please me a lot. Nonetheless, they still can't persuade me to read the latest chapters. I have to admit I don't have enough guts to do it. Fair enough, I will finish the comic someday---but not now.
